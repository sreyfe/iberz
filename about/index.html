Hello! This is a database of translations into Yiddish put together by me, Jonah Lubin, for my Master’s thesis at the Freie Universität Berlin. At present, I have collected over 1,300 translations, all of which come from the Yiddish Book Center’s digital library. I have annotated these entries with data relevant to their status as translations: source text (of which there are over 1,200), original author, original language, translator. I have also begun to link these translations to their originals with Wikidata id’s.

To look at the code for this project, see here. The website is hosted using Github Pages. If you want to take a look at the data, you can find it here.

I will soon begin the process of expanding the database beyond the books from the Yiddish Book Center. If you know of a translation into Yiddish, or have found an error, or just want to say hello, I would love to hear from you! You can reach me at jonah0lubin [at] gmail.com.
